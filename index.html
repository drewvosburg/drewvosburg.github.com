A designer's job is to listen and learn.

Who is the design for? What are they doing? How should they feel? How long should it hold their attention? Who does the design represent? What story does it tell?

I design with care and attention to detail, informed by facts and feelings, conversation and intuition.

I am never satisfied with my work; I am always striving to improve.
